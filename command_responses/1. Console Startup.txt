First, the console must be activated. 

To do this, switch the terminal program to 1200, 8, N, 1.
Then send the following hex string to the battery:

Data: 7E 32 30 30 31 34 36 38 32 43 30 30 34 38 35 32 30 46 43 43 33 0D
or ~20014682C0048520FCC3 as a string followed by CR


Now switch to 115200,8,N,1 amd send:
Data: 0D 0A (aka CR LF)


The pylon> prompt appears, allowing you to enter commands


For Login Admin mode use : 
login debug
